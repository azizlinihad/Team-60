Logic Gates

What are logic gates?

Logic gates are electronic circuits that perform logical operations on one or more binary inputs to produce a single binary output.

What is the purpose of logic gates?

Logic gates are used to perform basic logical functions in digital circuits and are the building blocks of digital electronics.

Where are logic gates commonly used?

Logic gates are used in microprocessors, memory devices, and circuits.
